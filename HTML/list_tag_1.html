Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup blueberrier
Directions:
  1. Mix flour and sugar together
  2. In a saparate bowl, mix together milk and egg
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients together
  4. Mix in blueberrier
  5. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 17 minutes
Notes:
For Lemon Blueberry Muffins,add 1 tdsp lemon zest
